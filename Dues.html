HOA ASSESSMENT INFORMATION
The 2017 Annual Assessment rate was set at $100.00 per dwelling (home), and is due June 1.  You will receive a bill in the mail.  Balances not paid in full by July 1 began accruing 1.5% interest per month until the balance is paid in full.  If you are unsure how much you owe, you can  email our accountant.

PAYING DUES 
You may pay  online or with a check.  For your convenience, payments can deposited in the HOA drop box.  The drop box is located on Prairie Rose Lane between Sunflower and Dropseed, on the West side of the street.

DUES STATEMENTS / CLOSING LETTERS
If you need a dues statement / closing letter / current invoice from the PFHOA due to refinancing your mortgage or selling your property, please go here for more information.
